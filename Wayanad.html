Lakkidi

Lakkidi is a small hill station located in the Wayanad district of Kerala, India. It is a popular tourist destination due to its natural beauty and proximity to other attractions in the area. The hill station is situated at an elevation of about 2,300 ft above sea level, and is surrounded by hiking trails and local markets
